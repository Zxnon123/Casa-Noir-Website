Indulge. Unwind. Escape.

Welcome to our premium hookah and cigar lounge, where we serve handcrafted coffee, light meals, and indulgent desserts made to delight. From our spot atop Lakefield Junction, you can soak in sweeping views of the golf course and the charm of Lakefield, the perfect place to relax, connect, and unwind in style.

Our Story

We were born from a vision to bring the sophistication and energy of Dubai’s nightlife to Lakefield. Inspired by the city’s fusion of elegance and modern luxury, we have created a space where premium hookah, handcrafted coffee, light meals, and decadent desserts blend seamlessly into a refined lounge experience. Perched atop Lakefield Junction with sweeping views of the golf course and surrounding beauty, we are more than just a lounge; we are your escape into style, flavor, and atmosphere.
